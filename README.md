# Strategic-Product-Placement-Analysis
# `Product Positioning Analysis Using Tableau Project Overview` 

```
This project aims to investigate the relationship between product positioning,
sales performance, pricing strategies, promotional activities, and consumer
behavior using Tableau. The objective is to understand how various product
positioning strategies influence sales volume and customer purchasing decisions.
A retail company has collected data on product categories, product positions,
prices, competitor pricing, seasonal trends, promotional campaigns, consumer
demographics, and foot traffic. By transforming this data into interactive
Tableau visualizations, the project uncovers meaningful insights that help
identify the most effective product positioning strategies. These insights
support businesses in optimizing product placement, pricing, and promotional
activities to improve customer engagement and maximize revenue.
```

# `Project Objectives` 

- `Analyze the relationship between product positioning and sales performance.` 

- `Evaluate the impact of product placement on average sales volume.` 

- `Study the influence of competitor pricing on product sales.` 

- `Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.` 

- `Compare sales performance across different product categories.` 

- `Examine seasonal trends affecting product sales.` 

- `Measure the effectiveness of promotional campaigns.` 

- `Analyze the relationship between foot traffic and sales volume.` 

- `Build interactive dashboards using Tableau.` 

- `Provide business recommendations to improve product positioning and increase revenue.`
